SoundQR

Your voice, encoded in QR.

SoundQR Developer Website

Official developer information for SoundQR on Android.

SoundQR is an offline Android app that records short voice clips, compresses them with Codec2, encodes them into QR codes, and decodes scanned SoundQR payloads back into sound on the device.

SoundQR's core voice recording, QR encoding, QR decoding, local history, and optional encryption features are designed to work on-device.
